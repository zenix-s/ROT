# Rot of Time

Rot of time is a roguelite, where the player needs to conquer the tower.
An action-packed Roguelite Spellcaster where you ascend a perilous tower, mastering the arcane arts through sentient magical books.

## The Vision
In Tower of Grimoires, your power is bound to the book you carry. As a mage, you must climb floor by floor, surviving relentless hordes of creatures. The game focuses on strategic movement and build-crafting, where the synergy between your Grimoire's innate magic and the upgrades found during your ascent determines your fate.

## Core Gameplay

- Automated Spellcasting: Your character automatically unleashes magic based on your equipped Grimoire. Your mastery is tested through precise movement, dodging, and selecting the right upgrades at the right time.

- Vertical Progression: Each floor increases in difficulty and complexity. Players must navigate branching paths, choosing between rooms that offer immediate power-ups or those that provide long-term resources.

- Dynamic Synergies: Modify your spells during each run. Combine elemental effects and casting patterns to transform basic projectiles into devastating screen-clearing calamities.

## Grimoire & Meta-Progression

- Grimoire Evolution: Your Grimoire is a persistent artifact. Between runs, you can refine and evolve your books, increasing their rarity and unlocking new latent abilities.

- Rune Socketing: Customize your playstyle by inscribing permanent runes into your Grimoires to enhance base attributes like casting speed, mana efficiency, or elemental potency.

- The Sanctuary: Use materials recovered from the tower to upgrade your permanent hub, unlocking new magical disciplines and persistent buffs for your mage.

- Lore & World-Building: Uncover the mysteries of the tower and its inhabitants through collectible lore items, revealing a rich backstory that deepens your connection to the world.

- Achievements & Challenges: Complete specific in-game challenges to earn unique Grimoires and cosmetic items, encouraging replayability and mastery of different playstyles.
